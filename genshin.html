About this game

Set forth on a journey across a fantasy world called Teyvat. In this vast world, explore seven nations, meet a diverse cast of characters with unique personalities and abilities and fight powerful enemies together with them, all on the way during your quest to find your lost sibling. Wander freely, immersing yourself in a world filled with life, and let your sense of wonder lead you to uncover all of its mysteries. That is, until you are at long last reunited with your lost sibling and bear witness to the culmination of all things at the end of your journey.
